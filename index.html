📚 Calculation Breakdown

Your monthly carbon footprint is calculated based on:

  • Travel by Car: km/week × 4 × 0.21
  • Electricity: kWh/month × 0.5
  • Waste: kg/week × 4 × 0.18
  • Flights: (flights/year × 250) ÷ 12

🌳 Trees needed to offset: Total CO₂ ÷ 21
(Each mature tree absorbs ~21 kg CO₂/month).
